Map of Europe

This is a map of Europe. When you click on a country the bar chart updates to the selected country. The bar chart displays the life satisfaction, life expectancy and employment rate for the year 2017. In the dropdown menu you can chose to display the data for men of women. When you hover over a country, it displays the amount of people who live in that country.
